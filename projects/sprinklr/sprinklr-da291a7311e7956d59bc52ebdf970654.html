THE CONTEXT
This project was the outcome of my summer internship with Sprinklr in 2019. Sprinklr is an American B2B software company based in New York City that develops a SaaS customer experience management platform. Their clients include 46% of the Fortune 50, four of the top five U.S. banks, five of the top eight U.S. insurance companies, four of the top seven hospitality companies, nine of the top 15 global technology companies, the top CPG, retail, consumer electronics, and sportswear brands.

WRT design, Sprinklr is a design driven firm, with the design team working in close collaboration with PMs in the end-to-end development of their products, and reporting diretly to the CTO of the company. Sprinklr offers multiple products for their clients, and as an intern in 2019, my role was the end to end conceptualisation and design of a new product
THE PRODUCT
An AI powered persona generator, which helps brands uncover dynamic, up-to-date data about their current audience, across all channels, to get the most precise audience persona based on all the engagement data.

The information can be leveraged to:
A) Curate content that resonates with their audiences, focus on them and provide only the most relevant content tailored to their exact values, interests, and needs.
B) Find the Right Influencer
Product
ANALYZE
Search and analyze within your own audiences for niche groups.
SEARCH RESULTS
Explore the different content suggestions based on your search terms and receive multiple aggregated personas to further drill down upon
PERSONA
Drill down into a particular persona group to then explore the nature of content that would appeal to them, and the major influencers this persona group is affected by.
Research
METHOD
Owing to the time limits of the internship period, it was decided that major focus would be on secondary research, with primary research limited to unstructed interviews with produt managers. I first went about understanding the different terminology and concepts in the domain, and then continued onto market research and competitor analysis
COMPETITOR
ANALYSIS
Studied the flows of major competitors to understand features and gaps to work upon.
The competitors chosen to study in depth about were Brandwatch and Audiense.com.
ANALYSIS
Drilled down on data points to analyze for creating personas, understood gaps in current systems, helped in visualizing use cases
ANALYSIS
From the deepened understanding of the system gained after the competitor analysis, I drew up use cases to understand the niche usages of the product and thereby helping me decide what features I might want to incorporate
Design

DIGITAL WIREFRAMES
Drawing wireframes on paper limited me as I couldn’t dynamically make changes and for each change, a whole new wireframe had to be drawn up
VERSION 1
First attempt at converting the wireframes into UI designs.
THE FLOW
After receiving feedback, I went back to the designs to fix the flow of the interface, information structure, persona cards, and the search experience. To fix this, I drew a task flow diagram to fix on the flows and function loop in the interface.
