Overview
THE PROJECT
CovidWire was a self initiated project started in April, with the aim of delivering high quality summarized news related to Covid 19 to the average Indian in their own language, in a quickly shareable format. Within a few weeks, we expanded our reach to include volunteers to help curate, summarize and translate news articles. The project also received media coverage

PROBLEMS
A weeks after the world became home-ridden because of Covid, we started observing a few trends in the way Covid news was presented, consumed and understood. We further had conversations with our frieds and family to further understand this space. From on these talks, we got a few insights into the problems that currently existed in the news delivery space
